Green Milk Tea

Green milk tea is a harmonious blend of earthy green tea and creamy milk, creating a soothing and refreshing beverage & it is healthy drinks for our body Even it is popular in Asian Cuisines.

Caramel Milk Tea

Caramel milk tea blends the rich, creamy goodness of milk tea with the indulgent sweetness of caramel. Its harmonious mix creates a delightful sip that's both comforting and decadent.

Earl Grey Milk Tea

Earl Grey Milk Tea blends the floral notes of Earl Grey tea with creamy milk for a delightful and aromatic beverage. Its unique combination offers a sophisticated twist on traditional milk tea.

WinterMelon Tea

Winter melon tea is a refreshing beverage made from the fruit of the winter melon, known for its subtle sweetness and cooling properties. This aromatic tea is popular in Asian cuisines.
